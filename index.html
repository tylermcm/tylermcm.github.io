About us

Founded in 2001 Heavenly Aromatherapy is a haven for those seeking peace and spiritual renewal. For over two decades, we’ve provided a serene space filled with carefully selected gifts, incense, essential oils, and a variety of candles, including top brands like Aloha Bay, Kringle, and Woodwick.
